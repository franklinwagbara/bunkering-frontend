Steps to apply for Survey Pressure Proposal
  1. Download Template A (Excel) file above.
  2. Fill the excel sheet.
  3. Click on the Apply button and upload the filled Template A (Excel) file.
  4. Click on submit and continue button.
  5. Make your REMITA payment.
  6. Submit your proposal.
  7. Please not that your Excel version should be Excel 2007 and above.
