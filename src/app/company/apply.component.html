Steps to apply for Survey Pressure Proposal

  • Download Template A (Excel) file above.
  • Fill the excel sheet.
  • Click on the Apply button and upload the filled Template A (Excel) file.
  • Click on submit and continue button.
  • Make your REMITA payment.
  • Submit your proposal.
  • Please not that your Excel version should be Excel 2007 and above.
